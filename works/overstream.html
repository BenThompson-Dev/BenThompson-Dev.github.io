Team Fortress 2 King of the Hill Map

Game / Level Design

For my Multiplayer Level Design university module, I designed and created a King of the Hill level for the game Team Fortress 2, using the Hammer level editor.

I designed the map to be symmetrical, making use of multiple levels and branching paths to balance an even playing field for all of the game's 9 classes. While keeping the branching paths, I wanted the map to stay quite open and maneuverable, allowing for players to quickly react to the changes in gameplay.

The level is available to download on its Steam Workshop level, where it can be played in-game.
