Hi! I'm Ben Thompson, a 2nd year Computer Games Design and Programming Student at Staffordshire University. I have experience in games design as well as games programming, having experience using C# in Unity and the blueprint system in Unreal Engine 4 both inside and outside my university assignments. I have a keen passion for games and have taken part in multiple game jams on top of my university projects.

I also have a strong passion for composing, and write music in my spare time, as well as incorporating it into my projects in various ways.

Currently, I'm working on my second year university modules: making a rhythm top-down shooter in Unity, developing a local multiplayer parkour prototype using UE4's blueprint and building a basic physics engine using C++ and DirectX.
